## CodeSignal Sim 3 Patterns
#678 Valid Parentheses *:
  Track lo/hi range of open parens
  lo = min possible, hi = max possible
  '*' expands range both ways
  Valid if lo==0 at end!

#238 Product Except Self:
  Left pass: prefix products
  Right pass: multiply by suffix
  O(n) time, O(1) extra space!

#340 K Distinct Characters:
  Sliding window + Counter
  Shrink when distinct > k
  Classic template problem!
